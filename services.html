Equipment Expertise

All Assembly Types Serviced

We test, repair, and install every type of backflow prevention assembly used in Virginia — residential to industrial.

RPZ

Reduced Pressure Zone Assembly

Highest level of protection. Required for high-hazard applications including chemical facilities, irrigation, and fire suppression.

DCVA

Double Check Valve Assembly

Used for medium-hazard connections. Common in commercial buildings, boilers, and non-chemical irrigation systems.

PVB

Pressure Vacuum Breaker

Most common device for residential irrigation systems. Must be installed above the highest downstream outlet.

AVB

Atmospheric Vacuum Breaker

Non-testable device used for low-hazard applications such as hose bibs and individual outlets.

SVB

Spill-Resistant Vacuum Breaker

Updated alternative to the standard PVB. Offers the same protection with reduced risk of discharge at the relief port.
