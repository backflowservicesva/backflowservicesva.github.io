What to Have Ready When You Reach Out

Having the following information on hand will help us get you scheduled as quickly as possible:

  • Your service address
  • Property type (residential, commercial, HOA)
  • Type of service needed (test, repair, install)
  • Assembly type if known (RPZ, PVB, DC, etc.)
  • Your water authority (e.g. Henrico, City of Richmond)
  • Any compliance notice you've received
  • Preferred date or urgency level
  • Number of assemblies to be tested

🚨 Received a compliance notice from your water authority? Don't risk shutoff.
